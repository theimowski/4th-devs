Style definition (use verbatim in every image prompt):
- Medium: minimalist instructional sketch (clean line drawing)
- Rendering: thin-to-medium charcoal/ink lines, no shading, no textures
- Palette: black ink lines on pure white background (#ffffff)
- Detail level: simplified human figure with clear limb angles and stance width
- Diagram conventions: subtle ground line; no text in the image
- Framing: full-body, centered, with some margin; consistent scale across poses
