# Gmail MCP Server — Use Cases

Example user queries an AI assistant with Gmail access would receive.

## Finding & scanning

1. Do I have any unread emails from my boss?
2. Show me emails from last week with attachments
3. Find all emails about the Q3 invoice
4. Are there any drafts I forgot to send?
5. What landed in my inbox today?
6. Search for the email where Sarah shared the API credentials
7. How many unread emails do I have right now?

## Reading content

8. What does that email from Anna actually say?
9. Show me the full body of the latest email from support@stripe.com
10. What's in my draft reply to Tom?

## Thread context

11. Show me the full conversation about the product launch
12. What did Mark reply to my proposal?
13. Catch me up on the hiring thread with HR
14. Who said what in that budget discussion?

## Attachments

15. Download the PDF Anna sent me yesterday
16. What files were attached in the onboarding email?

## Sending & replying

17. Send a quick email to tom@company.com saying I'll be late
18. Reply to Anna's last email and say I approve the budget
19. Forward that invoice email to accounting@company.com
20. Draft a reply to the client asking for a deadline extension

## Organizing

21. Mark all emails from newsletters as read
22. Archive everything older than 30 days in promotions
23. Label that thread as urgent
24. Move the Stripe email to trash

## Cross-cutting / compound

25. Summarize my unread emails and tell me which ones need a reply
26. Find the tracking number in my latest Amazon order email
27. Did anyone reply to the email I sent to the team on Monday?
28. Remind me — what date did the landlord say the lease expires? It was in an email last month
29. Check if David ever confirmed attendance for Friday's meeting
30. What's the last thing I sent to the client and did they respond?
