---
title: Good to Great
description: Jim Collins' study of companies that made the leap from good to enduring greatness, with practical frameworks for leadership and execution.
date: 2026-03-03
---

Jim Collins' *Good to Great* looks at why a small set of companies outperformed their peers for years and what patterns they shared. Collins argues that disciplined people, disciplined thought, and disciplined action - rather than charisma or big launches - drive durable results. The book introduces memorable frameworks like Level 5 Leadership, the Hedgehog Concept, and the Flywheel to describe how organizations build momentum. It's best read as a set of management lenses you can apply to your own team's strategy, culture, and execution.
