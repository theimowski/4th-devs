---

*Great by Choice* (Jim Collins and Morten T. Hansen) studies "10X" leaders and companies that outperformed their peers in turbulent, unpredictable environments. The core message is that exceptional results aren’t primarily about boldness or visionary risk-taking; they come from consistent execution (the **20 Mile March**), disciplined experimentation ("**fire bullets, then cannonballs**"), and preparing for shocks (**productive paranoia**) while sticking to a stable set of operating principles (a **SMaC recipe**). It’s a practical playbook for designing teams and systems that make steady progress when conditions are volatile.
