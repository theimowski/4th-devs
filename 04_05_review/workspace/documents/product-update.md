# Spring Product Update

This quarter we moved very fast and the platform is now in a **much better place** for every kind of customer that we have now and might have later.

The new dashboard gives teams a central place to see tasks, blockers, notes, and experiments without clicking through too many unrelated screens. See the [dashboard docs](https://example.com/docs/dashboard) for details.

We also improved the **onboarding flow** and that should make activation *way better* because users now understand the value much sooner in most situations.

## What changed

- The dashboard loads in one place instead of spreading the work across many tabs.
- New users see a shorter setup path with fewer decisions up front.
- Team activity now appears in a single stream that is easier to scan quickly.
- The `session_timeout` config now defaults to `30m` instead of `15m`.

## Rollout notes

We expect support volume to go down immediately after release because the flow is simpler now.

The launch will happen soon after final checks and the whole team will keep an eye on things if ~~anything weird~~ something unexpected happens.

> "Ship it when it's ready, not when the calendar says so." — internal motto
